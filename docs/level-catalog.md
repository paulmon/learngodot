# Level Catalog

This catalog maps the 12-level pack to the intended progression concepts from A4.

## Intro Push (Levels 1-3)
- `level01.txt`: one-crate straight push tutorial.
- `level02.txt`: two-crate basics with open planning space.
- `level03.txt`: one-crate repositioning with slightly larger board.

## Corridor Pressure (Levels 4-6)
- `level04.txt`: introduces ordering for two crates and two goals.
- `level05.txt`: corridor and choke-point pressure with side routing.
- `level06.txt`: constrained mid-board routing and crate sequencing.

## Multi-Crate Planning (Levels 7-9)
- `level07.txt`: three-crate planning with partial bottlenecks.
- `level08.txt`: three-crate planning with mixed corridor/open zones.
- `level09.txt`: wider board and longer reposition paths.

## Tight-Space Endgame (Levels 10-12)
- `level10.txt`: compact interior walls force careful final positioning.
- `level11.txt`: four-crate sequencing in denser obstacle layout.
- `level12.txt`: five-crate endgame requiring sustained move planning.

## Notes
- Every level uses enclosed borders (`#`) and balanced crate/goal counts.
- Use `docs/playtest-notes.md` to log soft-lock findings and tune at least 3 levels from feedback.
